Information About Owls

Owls are very interesting birds,and one that many other birds are afraid of.In fact fake owls are placed on top of the buildings to keep pigeons and other types of birds making nest there.The owl is said to be a symbol of wisdom and they make a unique sound at night that is distincily idintifiable.

The owl is one of the species of birds that you don't see very often though,but only hear.This is due to the fact that the owl isn't a bird during day.They are known to be very active only at night.There are two main categories of owls-barn owls and typical owls.

You will find a quite difference when it comes to the size of different species of owls out there.Some of them are only 4 inches when fully mature with the largest ones being about 28 inches fully grown. The vary in colour too depending on the regions where they live.They blend in well with there surrounding which makes it hard to spot them unless you are avidly searching for them in a given environment
