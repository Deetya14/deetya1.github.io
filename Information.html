    About Owls

    Owl Evalution Owls are amazing birds and research show them to be highly adaptable to different environment and changes.As a result of this there is no doubt that they have been here for millon years.

    Owl Feeding The owl is very fierce and powerful hunter so most of the time they catch the prey.They will only consume live animals so if they come across remains that someone else has killed they will leave it alone.

    Owl Habitat Owls are very well known as being very versatile birds when it comes to their habitat.They are able to live in variety of locations and some of those may surprise you.

    Owl Predators Owls definitely are very independent birds in the wild,but they do have predators to worry about.They are considered to be top food chain element though so they don't have too many problems.

    Owl Reproduction For the most part owls are very isolated creatures.That all changes as spring approaches though because that is when mating can occur.
